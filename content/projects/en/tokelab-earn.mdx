## Overview

I'm the PM and UX/UI Designer for Tokelab Earn, an end-to-end investment and yield management platform with institutional custody via Fireblocks. It lets investors handle deposits, yields, and withdrawals in a simple, transparent way, while Tokelab's operations team runs everything from a backoffice panel. The goal is an institutional-grade experience that stays approachable for any user profile.

## Problem

Tokelab needed an investment platform that combined institutional custody security with a straightforward UX, without surfacing Fireblocks, multi-sig, webhooks, or KYC complexity to end users. At the same time, the operations team needed robust admin tools to manage investments, reporting, and automated subscriptions from one place.

## Process

### Step 1: Product discovery & scope definition
I mapped all critical use cases: home dashboard with an investment simulator, deposit flow with Fireblocks Vault Account creation, active investment management, withdrawals with multi-signature approval, Metamap KYC, and the financial admin dashboard. I defined full scope, prioritized by impact, and structured a five-phase roadmap across nine weeks.

![Product discovery and roadmap](/images/projects/tokelab-earn/step1.png)

### Step 2: UX design & user flows
I designed full flows for two personas: the end investor and the Tokelab admin. For investors: onboarding, deposit, investing with yield and term selection, tracking active positions, withdrawals, and profile with history. For admins: financial reporting, automated subscription management, and access control. The focus was keeping institutional complexity invisible to investors.

![UX design and full flows](/images/projects/tokelab-earn/step2.png)

### Step 3: Full UI design
I designed the full visual UI in Figma: investment dashboard with daily and cumulative metrics, deposit and withdrawal screens, investment simulator, notification inbox, profile with integrated KYC, and the financial admin panel. The look had to feel trustworthy and institutional without sacrificing simplicity.

![UI design in Figma](/images/projects/tokelab-earn/step3.png)

### Step 4: Development coordination
I led technical coordination across five build phases: AWS/SST infrastructure setup, Fireblocks SDK integration for vaults and webhooks, core invest and withdraw flows, the admin dashboard, and E2E QA before production deploy. I worked closely with the Tech Lead and Senior Fullstack to resolve friction between design and Fireblocks integration constraints.

![Phased development coordination](/images/projects/tokelab-earn/step4.png)

## Result

Platform in active development with delivery targeted around nine weeks. Scope includes the full investor web app, financial admin panel, a Fireblocks workspace with vaults and policies, technical documentation, and a user manual. Code and deliverables are owned by Tokelab.
