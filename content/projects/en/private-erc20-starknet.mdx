## Overview

I was the UX/UI Designer for Private ERC20 on Starknet, a WakeUp Labs initiative to define a standard for private tokens on Starknet that can meet regulatory requirements. The product enables confidential transfers between accounts—hiding balances, not addresses—while the token issuer retains control of viewing keys for compliance workflows.

## Problem

Using tokens for payments on-chain exposes everyone’s balances publicly. That creates real friction: salaries visible to the world, asset purchases linked to identity, and no baseline financial privacy. The design challenge was making a cryptographically heavy system—ZK proofs, viewing keys, and private notes—feel obvious and effortless for end users.

## Process

### Step 1 — Understanding the technical system
Before designing a single screen, I went deep on how the model works: private notes, viewing keys, when users need to generate a ZK proof, and the contract owner’s role in the regulatory flow. That foundation drove every UX decision.

![Technical system understanding](/images/projects/private-erc20-starknet/step1.png)

### Step 2 — UX design & user flows
I designed the core flows: viewing a private balance in the wallet, confidential sends, ZK proof generation for transfers, and the contract owner experience for managing viewing keys. The focus was privacy as the default—without extra friction for the user.

![UX design and flows](/images/projects/private-erc20-starknet/step2.png)

### Step 3 — UI design in Figma
I designed the full visual UI: private balance dashboard, confidential transfer screens, ZK proof confirmation states, and Starknet wallet integration. The look had to signal security and trust while staying crystal clear.

![UI design in Figma](/images/projects/private-erc20-starknet/step3.png)

### Step 4 — Prototype and demo
I built the navigable prototype publicly shared as the project demo, showing the end-to-end private token transfer flow on Starknet.

![Prototype demo](/images/projects/private-erc20-starknet/step4.png)

## Result

A published demo presented to the Starknet and Web3 community. The project lays groundwork for a regulatory-aware private token standard on Starknet, differentiated by hiding balances while keeping addresses visible.
