## Overview

I was the UX/UI Designer for Superchain Accounts, a Smart Accounts system developed by WakeUp Labs for the Optimism ecosystem. The project won a mission grant from the DAO and was deployed on Optimism Mainnet with integration for OP Mainnet and Base. My work covered the full experience design through to an interactive prototype validated with the community.

## Problem

The Superchain ecosystem lacked a unified way for users to accumulate achievements, points, and on-chain badges intuitively. The technical complexity of Smart Accounts, with gas abstraction, account recovery, and transaction bundling, made the experience inaccessible to non-technical users. The design challenge was making all that complexity disappear behind a simple, clear interface.

## Process

### Step 1: Technical Discovery & Design Specs
I worked alongside the technical team to understand the system in depth: SuperChain Points as non-transferable ERC-20 tokens, Badges as ERC-721 Soulbound, Raffle Tickets as ERC-1155, and SuperChain Accounts as dynamic ERC-721 NFTs reflecting user achievements. This understanding was the foundation for all design decisions.

![Technical discovery and design specs](/images/projects/superchain-accounts/step1.png)

### Step 2: UX Design & User Flows
I designed the full user flows: account creation, points accumulation, badge claims, raffle participation, and achievement display. The focus was on minimizing friction: enabling users with no Web3 experience to understand and use the product without technical knowledge.

![User flows and wireframes](/images/projects/superchain-accounts/step2.png)

### Step 3: UI Design & Figma Prototype
I designed the full visual interface in Figma and developed an interactive prototype shared with the Optimism community and Badgeholders for feedback before development. All designs were published as open source in the project repository.

![UI Design in Figma](/images/projects/superchain-accounts/step3.png)

### Step 4: Community Iteration
Based on feedback from Badgeholders and the Optimism community, with strong presence in Latin America as the initial testing market, I iterated the design with concrete improvements before the Mainnet launch.

![Feedback-based iterations](/images/projects/superchain-accounts/step4.png)

## Result

Product deployed on Optimism Mainnet with OP Mainnet and Base integration. WakeUp Labs committed to maintaining the project for 2+ years. The design was recognized by the community and the code is fully open source for the Superchain ecosystem.
