## Overview

I was the PM and UX/UI Designer for DAMM Capital, a DeFi asset management platform built under the Optimism as Venture Studio program. The product was built, tested, and shipped on Optimism Mainnet—not a demo, but a full production product. DAMM Capital is not a typical yield aggregator: it uses predefined mathematical models and historical backtesting to inform liquidity and portfolio behavior.

## Problem

DeFi asset management projects often stall at the concept stage due to technical, product, and operational barriers. DAMM Capital had a differentiated proposition—asset management driven by mathematical models, not short-term incentives—but needed an execution partner who could take the idea all the way to production: discovery, design, development, and Mainnet deployment.

## Process

### Step 1 — Product & Technical Discovery
I started with an end-to-end discovery process: understanding DAMM Capital’s business model, target users, fund interaction flows, and technical constraints on Optimism. I defined the product architecture and aligned scope with Venture Studio requirements.

![Product discovery and definition](/images/projects/damm-capital/step1.png)

### Step 2 — UX Design & User Flows
I designed the full platform experience: fund views, portfolio modules, deposit and withdrawal flows, and account abstraction via Privy to support both crypto-native users and people new to Web3. The focus was making financial complexity approachable and clear.

![UX design and user flows](/images/projects/damm-capital/step2.png)

### Step 3 — Full UI design
I designed the complete visual interface in Figma—funds dashboard, portfolio views, smart contract interaction modules, and all UI states. The aesthetic had to signal trust and institutional quality without sacrificing clarity.

![UI design in Figma](/images/projects/damm-capital/step3.png)

### Step 4 — Development coordination & Mainnet testing
I coordinated frontend integration with ERC-7540 smart contracts, oversaw development, and led end-to-end testing on Optimism Mainnet. After testing, I handed off the product to the DAMM Capital team for production deployment.

![Testing on Optimism Mainnet](/images/projects/damm-capital/step4.png)

## Result

A complete DeFi platform delivered and publicly live on Optimism Mainnet. DAMM Capital launched its OP-denominated asset management product after completing the engagement with WakeUp Labs—a concrete example of a real product, not a demo, taken from idea to production within the grant timeframe.
