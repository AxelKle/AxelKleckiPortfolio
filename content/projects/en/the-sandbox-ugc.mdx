## Overview

I led product strategy and UX design for a user-generated content (UGC) platform proposed to The Sandbox DAO. The goal was to build a space where independent creators could upload, manage, and monetize their experiences within The Sandbox ecosystem, with fair visibility and $SAND incentive mechanisms.

## Problem

The Sandbox lacked a dedicated platform where independent creators could publish and discover experiences outside the official client. Visibility was limited, there were no advanced discovery filters, and no incentive mechanism for users to actively participate in rating and testing experiences.

## Process

### Step 1 — Discovery & Research
I conducted user interviews to validate flows and identify real friction points. I defined the system architecture, data structure, and technical specifications. I also researched smart contract integration possibilities for $SAND reward mechanisms.

### Step 2 — Design & Prototyping
I designed prototypes in Figma and presented them to The Sandbox DAO team for feedback before starting development. The design had to align with The Sandbox branding and be fully responsive — mobile and desktop.

### Step 3 — Product Definition
I defined a revenue model where creators can pay in $SAND to feature their experiences, and incentivize users to test and leave reviews. This generated revenue for the DAO treasury. I established acceptance criteria, development milestones, and governance rules for the project as a DAO open project.

### Step 4 — Roadmap & Delivery Planning
I structured the project into 4 milestones over ~5 months: Discovery → Backend → Frontend → Deploy & Testing. I coordinated with the WakeUp Labs technical team (2 Senior Full Stack, 1 Tech Lead, 1 QA, 1 Designer) to ensure viability and alignment with timelines committed to the DAO.

## Result

Proposal approved by The Sandbox DAO for development. The project establishes WakeUp Labs as a reference builder in the Web3 ecosystem, adding to a portfolio that includes clients like Arbitrum, Optimism, Coinbase, and Num Finance.

## Stack

React.js · Daisy UI · Framer · Node.js · PostgreSQL · Solidity · AWS · Google Analytics
