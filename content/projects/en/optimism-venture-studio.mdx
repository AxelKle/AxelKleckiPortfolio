## Overview

I was the Product Manager for the Optimism as Venture Studio program, an initiative funded by the Optimism Collective and run by WakeUp Labs. The goal was to shorten the path from idea to impact for high-potential teams building in the Optimism ecosystem, removing technical and operational barriers for projects in DeFi, gaming, and infrastructure.

## Problem

Many strong ideas inside Optimism never reach production because teams lack technical, strategic, or operational resources. The ecosystem needed more than advisory or grants alone: a true execution partner working side by side with selected teams from discovery through Mainnet deployment.

## Process

### Step 1: Selection mechanism design
I designed the screening and selection process: evaluation criteria, program structure, success metrics, and the support framework for each selected team. The focus was identifying teams with high onchain impact potential, considering deployed smart contracts, transaction volume, unique wallets, gas usage, and long-term ecosystem alignment.

![Selection mechanism definition](/images/projects/optimism-venture-studio/step1.png)

### Step 2: Project selection
I led the open call, evaluation, and selection of subsidized projects. I coordinated screening with the WakeUp Labs team and the Optimism Collective to stay aligned with program objectives.

![Project selection process](/images/projects/optimism-venture-studio/step2.png)

### Step 3: End-to-end execution and support
For each selected project I coordinated full technical and strategic support: product discovery, design, development, and deployment on Optimism Mainnet. One concrete delivery was DAMM Capital, a DeFi asset management platform taken from concept to production within the grant timeframe.

![Execution and project support](/images/projects/optimism-venture-studio/step3.png)

### Step 4: Reporting and public closure
I managed milestone tracking, progress documentation, and public results communication on the Optimism governance forum. The program includes a long-term commitment with post-delivery follow-up.

![Program reporting and closure](/images/projects/optimism-venture-studio/step4.png)

## Result

The program concluded with selected projects executed and shipped on Optimism Mainnet. DAMM Capital was a concrete case: a full DeFi product brought to production. WakeUp Labs' Venture Studio model proved effective at turning ideas into real onchain products within the Optimism ecosystem.
