## Overview

I led product strategy and UX/UI design for Propertize, a real world asset tokenization platform developed by WakeUp Labs. The product enables defining, issuing, and managing tokens representing real or digital assets, with a web app for end users and a backoffice panel for Propertize's internal team operations.

## Problem

Tokenizing real world assets is a technically complex process involving smart contracts, KYC/AML, on/off-ramp, and regulatory compliance. The core challenge was making all that complexity invisible to the end user: enabling someone with no technical knowledge to register, buy tokens, and manage their assets without friction. At the same time, Propertize's operations team needed efficient backoffice tools to manage assets, users, and metrics from a single place.

## Process

### Step 1: Discovery & Product Definition
I started with a discovery process to understand key use cases: token issuance, buy/sell, asset management, and operational administration. I defined the full functional scope, what went into the MVP and what stayed out, and documented technical specifications with the Tech Lead to align design and development from day one.

![Discovery and product definition](/images/projects/propertize/step1.png)

### Step 2: UX Design & Wireframes
I designed wireframes for each key use case: registration and onboarding flow, token buy and sell, portfolio management, and the administration panel. The focus was on reducing friction at each step and making flows clear for both end users and the operations team.

![Wireframes and user flows](/images/projects/propertize/step2.png)

### Step 3: UI Design & Prototype
I designed the full visual interface in Figma: web app for end users and backoffice for the Propertize team. I developed a navigable prototype to validate flows with the client before development, and prepared the technical handoff for the engineering team.

![UI Design and navigable prototype](/images/projects/propertize/step3.png)

### Step 4: Technical Team Coordination
I worked side by side with the Tech Lead and Senior Fullstack Engineer during development, resolving implementation questions, adjusting designs to real technical constraints, and ensuring smart contract integration aligned with the defined user experience.

![Development team coordination](/images/projects/propertize/step4.png)

## Result

Platform delivered. The product includes a web app for end users, a full backoffice panel for Propertize, and 12 hours monthly post-production support. Code and deliverables are client property.
